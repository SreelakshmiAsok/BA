Risk Segmentation Breakdown

Team 10

Customer Risk Tiers

High Risk

Probability > 70%

Typical Profile Characteristics

  • Month-to-month contracts
  • Very short tenure (often < 6 months)
  • Experiencing high monthly charges
  • Frequently missing value-add services like Tech Support
Urgent Action Required

Deploy immediate targeted discount offers (15-20% off next 3 months) or mandate proactive outreach from Customer Success.

Medium Risk

Probability 30% - 70%

Typical Profile Characteristics

  • Uncommitted to long term but past initial drop-off point
  • Moderately satisfied but highly price sensitive
  • Often using basic services without bundles
Increase Lock-in

Incentivize structural changes. Offer free trial upgrades to premium services (Streaming/Security) to build dependency.

Low Risk

Probability < 30%

Typical Profile Characteristics

  • Long-term tenure (Loyal customers)
  • Locked into One or Two-Year contracts
  • Often subscribed to multiple bundled services
  • Automated payment methods
Maximize Value

No churn-prevention needed. Enroll in standard VIP loyalty programs to foster referrals and focus on cross-selling.
