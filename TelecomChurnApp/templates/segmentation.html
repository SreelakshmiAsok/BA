Risk Segmentation Breakdown

Team 10

Customer Risk Tiers

Following Sprint 10 objectives, customers are segmented into corresponding risk categories (High, Medium, Low) based on the logistic regression probability output. This allows the Business Analysts to design and deploy targeted retention actions.

High Risk

Probability > 70%

Typical Profile Characteristics

  • Month-to-month contracts
  • Very short tenure (often < 6 months)
  • Experiencing high monthly charges
  • Frequently missing value-add services like Tech Support or Securtiy
Urgent Action Required

Deploy immediate targeted discount offers (15-20% off next 3 months) or mandate proactive outreach from Customer Success within 24 hours to address root service grievances.

Medium Risk

Probability 30% - 70%

Typical Profile Characteristics

  • Uncommitted to long term but past initial drop-off point
  • Moderately satisfied but highly price sensitive
  • Often using basic services without bundles
Increase Lock-in

Incentivize structural changes. Offer free trial upgrades to premium services (Streaming/Security) to build dependency, or offer one-time rebates to migrate to 1-Year contracts.

Low Risk

Probability < 30%

Typical Profile Characteristics

  • Long-term tenure (Loyal customers)
  • Locked into One or Two-Year contracts
  • Often subscribed to multiple bundled services
  • Automated payment methods
Maximize Value

No churn-prevention needed. Enroll in standard VIP loyalty programs to foster referrals and focus standard marketing on cross-selling higher tier service expansions safely.
