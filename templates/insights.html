Key Churn Drivers

Visual presentation of trends identified during the EDA phase (Sprints 6-8) regarding disconnection from telecom services.

Churn by Contract Type
Month-to-month 42.7% Churn
One year 11.2% Churn
Two year 2.8% Churn
Short-term billing flexibility acts as a primary catalyst for telecom disconnection. Month-to-month users represent the highest risk group.
Churn Rate by Tenure
0-12 Months (New) 47.5%
13-36 Months 29.1%
36+ Months (Loyal) 14.4%
The highest risk of deserting occurs within the first year of acquisition, underscoring the necessity for robust early-retention initiatives.
